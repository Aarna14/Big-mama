Who We Are

Not just a cart.
A vibe.

Big Mama started as a small red cart parked right outside the college gate in Sonipat — and honestly, it never needed to be anything more. We make Chinese street food the way it was meant to be: fast, flavourful, and affordable enough that you don't have to text your dad for money.

From crispy chilli potatoes to steaming momos stuffed just right, every item is made fresh to order. No frozen stuff, no drama — just good food.
